From keyword to live, indexed site.

SiteButcher covers the entire pipeline of building a website at scale — not just generation. Each stage is automated, and you stay in control from one desktop board.

Build a semantic core

Start from a keyword engine that clusters terms by intent. Feed in your own keywords or let the engine assemble a structured core for the topic.

// local-first by design

Your data never touches our servers.

SiteButcher runs entirely on your own computer. We don't host your sites, store your keywords, or hold your credentials. There is no central database of your network — because there is no central database at all.

  • ✓ Generated sites, drafts and content stay in your local app folder
  • ✓ API keys and account credentials are stored only on your machine
  • ✓ Sites deploy directly from your computer to servers you own
  • ✓ No telemetry on your projects — your network is nobody's business but yours

// built for operators

Everything a multi-site operation needs.

Beyond generation, SiteButcher gives you the tooling to run a real network — accounts, domains, servers and monitoring, all in one place.

🗂️

Multi-account management

Connect many Google, Cloudflare and registrar accounts. Set defaults, switch, and distribute sites across them automatically.

🌐

Domain operations

Check availability, generate names, see live pricing and bulk-register domains through your registrar — then manage their DNS.

📦

Bulk pipelines

Queue large batches from a CSV and let them run. Shared settings with per-site overrides keep big runs consistent.

🖥️

Your own servers

Add multiple deploy targets, test connections, and watch deployments stream live as sites go out over SSH.

🔍

Search Console at scale

Verify, submit sitemaps and index across every connected Google account, with a unified performance dashboard.

📊

Live monitoring

Uptime, SSL and HTTP checks for every site, with one-click re-checks and Cloudflare status at a glance.

🎨

40+ visual styles

From clean and editorial to bold and neon — each site can look distinct, reducing repetitive footprints. The style library keeps growing.

🌍

Any language, RTL ready

Generate localized sites in any language you add, with proper right-to-left handling and a clear primary-language structure per site.
