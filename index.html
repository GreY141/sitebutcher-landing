// data & privacy

What SiteButcher accesses in your Google account.

SiteButcher uses Google APIs only to perform the actions you ask for inside the app. Access is limited to Search Console and site verification.

Google account access

When you connect a Google account, SiteButcher requests permission to: add and verify site ownership in Google Search Console; submit sitemaps and request URL indexing; read Search Console performance data (impressions, clicks, positions) to display in the dashboard; manage your sites' Search Console settings; and read your account email address to identify the connected account. This data is used only to operate features inside the app. It is never sold and never shared with third parties. SiteButcher's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
